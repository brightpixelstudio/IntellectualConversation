About Intellectual Conversation Group

We are a new group on Nextdoor called Intellectual Conversations.

No politics or religion, just topics focusing on Art, Philosophy, Psychology, History, Biology, Music, Technology, etc. I welcome everyone who enjoys these topics and others that are similar with insight/debate, but not arguments/judgments.

Our Members

We have some awesome users who offer extremely interesting topics that will expand your mind and provide new perspectives. I know it has mine.

We are local and located in Greenville/Simpsonville area. We meet in person about every 6 months to shake hands and connect with new people.

Join Our Group

We welcome everyone to join Intellectual Conversations!

To provide all of our members insight into topics that they may not know about, we ask that you join our group on Nextdoor. This will allow you to post topics and engage in intellectual discussions with other members. We look forward to seeing you there!
